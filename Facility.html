Our Facility

Accommodation

The hostel provides accommodation for 144 students,72 each for boys and girls. All the rooms are double seated and are fornished with ceiling fan,wardrobe table,chair & bed for each student.in addition,each hostel has a visitor's rooms every floor is provided with 4 common washrooms and toilets.all bathrooms of the hostel are fitted with both electric geysers and solar water heathing system.

Mess & Dining Hall

The mess is managed by a contractual agency recommened and approved by the hostel commitee. two separete dining halls are provided for boys and girls.meal timings are as follows:

Breakfast

8.00 am to 9.00 am

Lunch

1.00 pm to 2.00 pm

Dinner

8.00 pm to 9.00 pm

Reading Room

The hostel has two air-conditioned reading rooms(Separate for boys & girls). internet facilities are provided.a reading space can help build literacy skills such as sentence structure,punctuation and grammar.which is fantastic for developing important skills in reading and writing.

Sports & Entertainment

The hostel has two common rooms (Separates for boys & girls) where facilities for games like carrom board and chess,along with television,are provided.the hostel is close to the college playground which has a basketball court,a football field and practice pitches.besides,each hostel has a table tennis room.also a few gym equipment have been installed both girls and boys hostel for residents use.

Medical

Whenever needed residents may utilize the medical services provided at the W.U.S health center,located in south campus,University of Delhi.each resident is required to fill up the W.U.S helth center form and play a membership fee.the college nusre is available from 9.00 am to 4.00 pm on all working days.

In case of a medical emergency,a student is taken to the nearest hospitla and the local guardian is duly informed the local guardian is required to take charge thereafter and the entire responsibility for treatment and expenses will be borne by the parents/local guardian and the college will not br reponsible in any way for the further course of treatment. a student is requested to provide at the time of admission information about nay specific medical problems/ he she is known to suffer from.

Discipline And Security

  • Security guards are on duty 24X7 in both girls and boys hostel
  • CCTV cameras are installed at both the gates and outside the warden's office in both hostels.
  • Lockers provided for residents to keep their valuables safe.


  • Residents are allowed 4 late-night and 4 night-outs in a month.
  • Residents are required to record their exit form and enrty into the hostel in the registers.
