About Hostel

The J S Hostel College Hostel Located within the college campus was set up in 2008 .it consists of two blocks.at the J S hostel.we strive to giv the comforts of a home as well as the optimim ambience for fulfilling both academic and co-curricular of residents this home away from home is the hub of a harmonious co-existence of multicultural,multiethnic and multireligious students all representative of the J S character. the hostel provides accommodations for 144 students,72 each for boys and girls.

This on campus residentil facility is offered to full time undergradute student.only those student whose parents do not reside in or around the national capital region of Gujarat Due to the limited availability of accommodations,admission to the hostel is granted based on academic merit.the allocation of hostel rooms is decided by the hostel committee.the hostel administrative functionaries,in co-operation with the Hostel student association work towards the efficiend and smooth function of the hostel.they contribute to the manitenance of harmonious community life. the home that we offer to you,within the premises of the college,is major constituent of the college's effort at imparting a sensitive, well balanced education.
